How to use:

Rotation: Clicking outside of the cube and dragging will rotate the cube in 3D.

Twisting faces: Clicking any sticker and dragging it to a neighbouring sticker on the same face will twist the cube face along that axis.

Sticker images: The sticker images represent the following

  • Standard Pokemon sprite
  • Shiny Pokemon sprite
  • Type 1
  • Type 2 (if applicable)
  • Heavy or light (30kg threshold)
  • Highest stat
    • Attack
    • Special attack
    • Defense
    • Special defense
    • Speed
    • Health
